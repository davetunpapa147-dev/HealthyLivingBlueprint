The DASH diet (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension) is proven to lower blood pressure in clinical trials. This 7‑day plan shows how to implement DASH with recipes and a printable grocery list.

DASH principles at a glance

Emphasize fruits, vegetables, whole grains, low-fat dairy, lean proteins; limit sodium and processed foods.

7‑Day Meal Plan (example day and summary for rest)

Day 1 — Example

Breakfast: Overnight oats with berries and walnuts. Lunch: Grilled chicken quinoa salad. Dinner: Baked salmon with roasted broccoli and sweet potato. Snacks: apple and yogurt.

Days 2–7: rotate similar balanced meals; include legumes, low-fat dairy, whole grains, and a variety of vegetables.
